public EponymousTemplateDeclaration parseEponymousTemplateDeclaration()
Parses an EponymousTemplateDeclaration 
eponymousTemplateDeclaration:
   ('enum' | ($LITERAL 'alias')) Identifier templateParameters '=' assignExpression ';'
   ('enum' | ($LITERAL 'alias')) Identifier templateParameters '=' type ';'
   ;
